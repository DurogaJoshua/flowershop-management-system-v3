From a Small Shop to Your Heart

Maria's Floral Fantasy began as a small flower shop in Tacloban City, founded by Maria Santos with a simple dream: to bring happiness to people through beautiful, fresh flowers.

What started as a humble venture has blossomed into one of the most beloved floral destinations in the region, serving thousands of customers and becoming part of countless celebrations, milestones, and cherished memories.

Today, we continue Maria's vision of combining traditional Filipino hospitality with modern floral artistry, creating arrangements that speak the language of love, gratitude, and celebration.
