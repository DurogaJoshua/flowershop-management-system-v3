Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM within Tacloban City. For nearby areas, please allow 1-2 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, Maya, bank transfer, and all major credit cards. Payment is secure and hassle-free.

What is your return policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, contact us within 24 hours with photos. We offer replacements or refunds for damaged or incorrect items.

Can I request custom arrangements?

Absolutely! We love creating custom designs. Call us or visit the shop to discuss your vision with our florists.
